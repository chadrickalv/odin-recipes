Salmon & Rice

Home

Description

This dish is a classic. Easy to make. Enjoyable when finished. It can go with any toppings of your choice, goes extremely well with greens & will always leave you satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Greens
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat a pan over medium heat
  3. Season the salmon with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Cook the salmon for about 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  6. Slice the avocado and prepare the greens.
  7. Serve the salmon over rice, topped with avocado, greens, a squeeze of lemon, and chopped parsley.
  8. Enjoy your meal!
