Spaghetti with Tostones Recipe

Home

Description

This is a beautifully hand crafted dominican recipe. Loved by all & left fulfilled. Budget friendly, easy to make, & delicious.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Tostones (Fried Plantains)
  • Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
  • Lemon Juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Grab 2 platanos & cut the ends off. Only have the inside show slightly.
  5. Slice the platanos into 1-inch thick rounds.
  6. Fry the platanos in the pan until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Remove the platanos from the pan and flatten them with a tostonera or a meat hammer on flat surface.
  8. Return the flattened platanos to the pan and fry again until crispy.
  9. In the same pan, add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  10. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with garlic, tossing to coat.
  11. Season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
  12. Serve the spaghetti topped with grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, and a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.
  13. Enjoy your spaghetti with crispy tostones on the side!
