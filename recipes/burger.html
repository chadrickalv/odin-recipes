Burger Recipe

Home

Description

This burger is perfect for a quick at home healthy meal when you dont want to eat out. Budget friendly, easy to make, & delicious. Customizable with your favorite toppings & sauces.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Burger Buns
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form the mixture into patties.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side until they reach your desired doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your choice of sauces.
  8. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!
